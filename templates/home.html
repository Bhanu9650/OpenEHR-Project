About us

EAPR stands for Easy Access Patient Record, the objective of this project to bring out transparency at each stage of the medical process be it a patient , doctor, pharmacist. Any patient, doctor or pharmacist can register here to avail the services. The services that we provide are :-
The patient can register through patient Sign-Up and check his/her prescription.
The Doctor can record and manage all his patients data like prescription, medication, illness history, allergies etc.
The pharmacist would be able to get a digital format of prescription and can log the details of medicine prescribed by the doctor to patients.
